Rules of the Game


  • On first login, every user is credited with 1000 byteCoins (no pun unintended =P) in the wallet.
  • There are two types of leagues you can take part in, for each match
    • Win Predictor - where you predict the winners (along with goal margin)
    • Stat Guru - where you predict a few stats(Yes/No types) for a game
  • You can only bet 95% of your wallet balance at once
  • The bet amount would be deduced from your wallet when you place the bet
  • You can withdraw from any bet till 1 hour before the start of the game
  • At every frequent interval, you will be credited with byteCoins to your wallet. There will be other ways to earn the byteCoins as well which will be communicated later.
  • Winner and Runner-up to win Prizes
  • In case of a penalty shootout, the penalty goal difference will be considered.

DISCLAIMER : THIS IS AN INTERNAL FUN EVENT ONLY AND NO REAL BETTING OR MONEY IS INVOLVED.
